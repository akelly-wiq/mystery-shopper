# Mystery Shopping Agent - Executive Summary
**For Owen Lim | Friday, April 25, 2026**

---

## What We're Building
**AI system that automatically mystery shops like real customers**
- 8 shopping missions × 5 customer personas (CREST) × 3 retailers
- **= 120 automated mystery shops** available on demand
- Delivers daily competitive basket price comparisons

---

## Business Value
✅ **For CEO:** Daily competitive intelligence on Woolworths vs Coles vs Aldi basket pricing  
✅ **Customer lens:** See pricing through different CREST segment perspectives  
✅ **Automated:** No manual shopping required (vs Feb 20 one-time POC)  
✅ **Scalable:** Foundation for ongoing competitive monitoring  

---

## How It Works (Simple)
1. **Search:** Find products for each ingredient across all retailers
2. **Decide:** AI agents select products based on customer persona rules (Saver picks promotions, Traditional picks familiar brands)
3. **Validate:** System checks basket completeness and price accuracy
4. **Report:** Output comparison dashboards
