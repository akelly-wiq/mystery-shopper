- More accurate product predictions
- Category-specific decision rules
- Traditional will choose Woolworths milk, Leggo's sauce

## Key Insights from Enrichment

### 1. Refined are Premium Health Shoppers
- Highest health aisle frequency (71.7%)
- NOT because most health-conscious
- BECAUSE buying premium supplements, specialty products
- Spend more on health aisle than any segment

### 2. Traditional Own Brand is Category-Specific
- Highest own brand penetration (51.5%)
- But only for commodities
- Remain brand-loyal for taste-critical categories
- Pragmatic, not contradiction

### 3. Savers Use Convenience Most
- 79.7% buy ready meals (HIGHEST)
- Time-poor families need efficiency
- Will buy if value calculation works
- Batch cooking alternative

### 4. Conscious Avoid Convenience
- 34.8% NEVER buy ready meals (highest)
- Prefer control over ingredients
- Fresh > frozen unless clean ingredients
- Batch cook healthy meals

### 5. Essential Face Health Cost Barrier
- 67.8% say "health food too expensive" (HIGHEST)
- Want to be healthy but budget wins
- 52.6% rate diet as "not healthy"
- Pragmatic acceptance
