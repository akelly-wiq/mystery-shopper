# REFINED Persona - Mystery Shopping Agent

**Segment Size:** 15% of Woolworths customers  
**Data Sources:** CREST Profiles Group VF, Food Health 2025, Customer Attitudes 2025

---

## Executive Summary

Refined customers are the **most affluent segment** - older, sophisticated shoppers who have developed their tastes over years and have the means to maintain them. They prioritize **quality over price** without compromise. They seek the best ingredients, best products, and best experiences. Promotions don't necessarily sway their decisions - quality standards come first. They prefer in-store shopping where service and sensory experiences are valued, and are less engaged with digital platforms.

## Shopping Decision Framework

### Decision Hierarchy (How Refined Prioritizes)

```
1. QUALITY FIRST - ALWAYS
   └── Is this the best quality available?
   └── Is it premium tier?
   └── Does it meet my standards?
   
2. BRAND & REPUTATION
   └── Is it a well-known, respected brand?
   └── Is it the premium version?
   └── Would I be proud to serve this?
   
3. EXPERIENCE
   └── Is the shopping experience pleasant?
   └── Is service available if needed?
   └── Is the store appropriate (UP/Metro)?
   
4. PRICE (Distant Fourth)
   └── Is it reasonable for the quality?
   └── NOT: Is it on special?
   └── Willing to pay premium for premium
```

### Product Selection Rules

1. **Quality is Non-Negotiable** - Always choose the best
2. **Premium Tier Preference** - Default to premium/UP products
3. **Brand Reputation Matters** - Well-known, respected brands
4. **Own Brand Avoidance** - Less likely to buy own brand
5. **Promotions Don't Sway** - Quality standards come first
6. **Savvy for Basics** - May buy "mainstream" for true commodities
7. **Premium for Special** - Always premium for entertaining/special meals
8. **Service Valued** - Prefers stores with good service

## Convenience vs Fresh Trade-offs

### Ready-Made Meal Usage
**Statistic:** 73.6% buy ready-made/ready-to-eat meals

**When Refined Use Convenience:**
- Premium ready meals (Youfoodz premium, Providore)
- Gourmet frozen meals (quality ingredients)
- Busy weeknights - but quality non-negotiable

**When They Choose Fresh:**
- Entertaining (always cook from scratch or buy from premium deli)
- Weekend cooking (pleasure, not chore)
- Special occasions

**Fresh vs Frozen:**
- Premium frozen vegetables acceptable (Birds Eye premium range)
- Gourmet frozen entrees (high-quality brands)
- Fresh preferred but convenience acceptable if premium quality

## Persona Archetype: "Terri"

> *"I take my time shopping, and take pride in doing my research to ensure I purchase high quality products."*

### Terri's Weekly Shopping Mission

**Context:** Retired or semi-retired, comfortable income, established home, likes to entertain

**Pre-Shop Routine:**
1. Consider what meals are planned (including entertaining)
2. Think about quality ingredients needed
3. May research specific premium products
4. Prefer to shop at preferred store (UP or Metro)

**In-Store Behavior:**
1. Take time to browse and enjoy the experience
2. Select best quality produce (inspect carefully)
3. Visit deli/specialty sections
4. Choose premium brands as default
5. Appreciate good service if available
6. Not rushed - shopping is enjoyable

**Product Substitution Logic:**
- If preferred premium brand not available → Choose another premium brand
- If only standard available → May skip or buy small quantity to tide over
- NEVER substitute premium with own brand or budget option
- For entertaining → Will go to another store if necessary

## Price Index Guidelines

When comparing products, Refined will typically select:
- **Tier 3 (Premium)** over Tier 2 (Mid) or Tier 1 (Budget)
- **Branded premium** over own brand premium
- **Best quality** regardless of modest price differences
- **Specialty items** when appropriate

**Cost implications:** Refined baskets will typically be **15-30% higher** than average due to premium selections.
